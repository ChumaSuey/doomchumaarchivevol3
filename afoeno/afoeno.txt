Description             : Aesthetic Face of Evil was an independent project i
                          decided to start a while ago, since i've got
                          captivated by Neon Overdrive, i decided to give it
                          a go. (this WAD uses Neon Overdrive assets)
                          
                          Since my mapping style in Quake and also Doom has
                          leaned toward the Aestheticness and Groovy vibes,
                          it seemed a good step for me in doing so.
                          
                          The music was composed by me, no other original
                          music idea, i went to produce this track to insert
                          it in the map, a challenge because my MIDI music
                          composing has had ups and downs, but i'm always
                          willing to experiment, learn and grow.
                          
                          Hugs, hugs for everyone!
                          
                          Shine with style!
                          
                          - Chuma
